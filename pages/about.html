Creativity has always accompanied me, since I was a child when I was drawing until today with my profession. In the last few years I have worked hard to develop my skills as a graphic designer. I have done projects, internships, studied at university, worked with clients and worked for 3 years in a company where I was able to contribute significantly to its growth. Thanks to all these experiences that have accompanied me, I have become an organized and disciplined person who believes in working with objective purposes and structured routines always focused on the user.

My philosophy in design challenges the current parameters of visual systems,I look for innovation and functionality without leaving in lack of identity

In a world where everything looks similar, for me the fact is to find the core of a brand's essence and that with its identity it can translate its intentions. So that it can embrace the new parameters that arise in the world. Whether it's the way you see it, hear it or interact with it. I'm not just here to make it look pretty, I'm here to make it transcend.

HOW DO I APPLY IT?

1

Brand Strategy

Besides designing, I translate ideas. When it comes to strategy, I consider artistic direction, cultural influence, and data to support and justify decisions.

2

VISUAL IDENTITY

The story of each brand, besides being visually adapted, must be respected with its origin, narrative and influence. I strongly study the history of each brand so that its identity does not fall victim to one more dynamic trend, but continues to write its history.

3

VISUAL SYSTEM

Whether in a web, campaign or brand creation, each element that is developed must accompany in a coherent way the anatomy of the body of the brand. In this way, a harmony is built that helps the narrative to be achieved.

4

BRAND DEVELOPMENT

In a world of constant change it is important to keep the bar high for the brand, I do not seek to finish the job and say goodbye but to maintain a relationship with the project to continue building its message, evolve and transcend together.
